About Me

Peter Boy Wanga Junior is a Microsoft Azure Junior Cloud Administrator and aspiring Cloud Data Engineer based in Nairobi. He is passionate about cloud, data and building agile, accurate and effective solutions that create real value for businesses and communities.

He is currently pursuing a Computer Science degree at Multimedia University of Kenya, where he continues to strengthen his skills in cloud infrastructure, networking and data systems.

Peter has hands-on experience building Azure environments, including virtual networks with NICs, NSGs, virtual machines and configured policies to ensure secure and efficient communication across networks. His long-term goal is to become a Senior Cloud Data Engineer focused on designing scalable cloud and data solutions that solve real-world problems.

His greater cause and goal of operation is Women Empowerment. Growing up in a household with his mother and two sisters gave him a perspective he could not have gained elsewhere. He saw how women often face unfair treatment, limited opportunities and scarce resources despite their potential and contribution to society.

Because of this, he wants to build platforms where women are heard, valued and given opportunities to grow. He believes technology and data can help expose inequalities, identify gaps and support better decisions that lead to practical and measurable change.

This is why he chose Data Engineering. He wants to quantify problems through data, organize information into understandable and accurate systems and help organizations and communities make informed decisions based on evidence rather than assumptions.

Outside technology, Peter enjoys running and working out because he has a lot of energy to channel and training helps him stay focused and sleep well at night.
